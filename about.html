About Rabbi's Garden

Mike Wuertemburg is the creator and owner of Rabbi's Garden. Also founder and President of Equip Ministries along with his wife Edith Wuertemburg.

This ministry began in August of 2020 in Fruita, Colorado, USA. Being led by The Holy Spirit I envisioned reaching many nations and encouraging people to grow in their relationship with Yeshua (Jesus).

Becoming more intimate with Him. To increase their hunger for the Word and to bless others through their insights to The Creator of the universe YHWH.

Our Core Values

Faith

Our organization is rooted in faith, trusting in God's plan for our lives and our work, knowing that with unwavering belief in His power and promises, we can overcome any obstacle and fulfill our calling.

Love

At the heart of our Christian organization is the value of love, as we believe that loving others unconditionally, just as Christ loved us, is the foundation of all our actions and relationships.

Stewardship

Stewardship is central to our mission, as we recognize the responsibility to wisely manage the resources God has entrusted to us, whether that be time, finances, or talents, ensuring they are used to further His Kingdom.

Our Sacred Spaces

By faith and your support, these sanctuaries will stand as a blessing for generations.

Under Construction – Witness the hope and vision behind these sacred spaces.

Meditation Sanctuary

We hope to soon begin preparing the foundation for this tranquil space, thoughtfully designed for prayer and contemplation. With your help, we'll bring it to life complete with water features and lush plantings that will make it a true sanctuary.

Your support will provide: Native plants, benches, and a flowing water feature to enhance meditation.

Biblical Oasis

We hope to begin planting the first biblical species soon, as volunteers prepare to create educational displays. Contributions will help us acquire more plants mentioned in scripture and create interpretive signage.

Your support will provide: Olive trees, fig trees, and educational materials about their biblical significance.

Community Blessing

We hope to soon see the framework of our community garden take shape, with neighbors coming together to build raised beds. Your donations will help us complete the irrigation system and provide tools for the volunteers who will bring this vision to life.

Your support will provide: Irrigation system, gardening tools, and starter plants for the community.

Healing Haven

We hope to soon begin installing the first accessible pathways that will wind through this therapeutic space. With your support, we'll be able to add fragrant plants, tactile elements, and quiet seating areas designed for reflection and restoration.

Your support will provide: Accessible pathways, aromatic herbs, and braille signage for visitors.

Prayer Pathway

We hope to soon begin marking the outline of our prayer labyrinth, with volunteers preparing the base layer. Your contributions will help us complete the stone pathway and surrounding plantings, creating a sacred space for reflection and spiritual journey.

Your support will provide: Stone pathway materials and drought-resistant border plants.

Memorial Garden

We hope to soon begin clearing the site for our memorial garden, preparing the ground for meaningful perennial plantings. With your support, we'll install commemorative plaques and create a peaceful setting for remembrance and reflection.

Your support will provide: Memorial plaques, perennial flowers, and quiet seating areas.

Our Divine Impact

At Rabbi's Garden, we believe that the Word of God is a transformative force capable of touching every heart and every community. Our divine impact is rooted in the power of the Gospel, through which we aim to uplift, guide, and inspire individuals to live with purpose, faith, and love.

We are committed to sharing the message of salvation, healing, and hope, with a focus on making disciples who reflect Christ's love in their everyday lives. By embracing the teachings of the Bible, we seek to create an environment where people can encounter the divine presence of God and experience the transformation that comes from a deeper relationship with Him.
