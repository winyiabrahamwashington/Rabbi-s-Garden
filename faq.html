Frequently Asked Questions

About Rabbi's Garden

What is Rabbi's Garden?

Rabbi's Garden is a Christian ministry dedicated to teaching the Scriptures from a Hebraic perspective, focusing on the language, culture, and context of the Bible. Our mission is to help believers deepen their relationship with Yeshua (Jesus) and expand the Kingdom of YHWH on earth.

Who founded Rabbi's Garden?

Rabbi's Garden was founded by Mike Wuertemburg, along with his wife Edith Wuertemburg. Mike is also the founder and President of Equip Ministries.

What makes Rabbi's Garden different from other ministries?

Rabbi's Garden emphasizes:

  • Teaching Scripture from its original Hebrew context
  • Exploring the Jewish roots of Christianity
  • Focusing on the cultural background of biblical texts
  • Encouraging deeper intimacy with Yeshua
  • Providing practical applications for modern believers

Beliefs and Teachings

What are the core beliefs of Rabbi's Garden?

Our core beliefs include:

  • The Bible as the inspired Word of God
  • Yeshua (Jesus) as Messiah and Son of God
  • Salvation through faith in Yeshua
  • The importance of understanding Scripture in its original context
  • The ongoing work of the Holy Spirit in believers' lives
  • The unity of Jewish and Gentile believers in Messiah
Why focus on the Hebraic understanding of Scripture?

Understanding the Hebrew context of Scripture helps us:

  • Grasp the original intent of biblical authors
  • Appreciate the cultural background of biblical events
  • See connections between the Old and New Testaments
  • Understand Jewish customs and traditions mentioned in Scripture
  • Gain deeper insights into Yeshua's teachings

This approach enriches our faith without compromising the gospel message.

Do you follow Jewish traditions or laws?

We study Jewish traditions to better understand Scripture, but we believe that salvation comes through faith in Yeshua alone, not through observance of the Law. We respect Jewish traditions as valuable for understanding biblical context, but we don't require observance of them for salvation or Christian living.

Technical Questions

How do I access your video content?

Our video content is available through:

  • Our website's "Sermons" section
  • Our YouTube channel (@mikewuertemburg2204)

All videos are free to access, though some may have accompanying study materials available for purchase.

Why can't I play some videos?

If you're having trouble playing videos, try these solutions:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Update your browser or try a different browser
  • Clear your browser's cache and cookies
  • Try accessing from a different device
  • Ensure your ad blocker isn't interfering
  • Check YouTube's status if the problem persists

If issues continue, contact us with details about the problem.

How do I reset my password for the shop?

To reset your shop account password:

  1. Go to the Shop page and click "Login"
  2. Click "Forgot Password"
  3. Enter the email address associated with your account
  4. Check your email for a password reset link
  5. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password

If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact us for assistance.
